GEN-Z CARS, founded in 2020 by Salman Alvi, a young entrepreneur and car enthusiast based in New York City, is dedicated to manufacturing premium quality component cars. As a sister company to Alvi Motorsports, GEN-Z CARS specializes in producing modern reinterpretations of iconic vehicles such as the GT40 and Cobra, along with developing unique designs of their own.

True to its name, GEN-Z, the company blends vintage race car aesthetics with cutting-edge technology to create vehicles that are not only true to their heritage but also suitable for daily driving. GEN-Z CARS aims to uphold the integrity of classic race cars while making them more accessible to a broader audience.

One of GEN-Z CARS' primary goals is to deliver the highest quality vehicles at cost-effective prices, allowing more racing enthusiasts to enjoy these exceptional cars. All vehicles are meticulously crafted at our facility in South Carolina by a team of automotive experts, experienced designers, and skilled fabricators.
